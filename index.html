STEM Inclusivity Lab

A multilingual and collaborative open source platform providing resources to advance underrepresented groups in STEM

Get Started

For STEM to evolve it needs to become more inclusive

STEM fields have long been dominated by a very homogenous population. For exponential technological growth to support social change and not further marginalize certain groups, it needs to become more diverse and inclusive.

STEM Inclusivity lab was created to support the active engagement of marginalized groups in STEM by providing open-source documentation for educators and youth leaders who aim to create inclusive spaces for these groups to get excited about STEM.

  • Create a community of like-minded individuals
  • Empower marginalized groups to pursue careers in STEM
  • Share the valuable resources to encourage youth and educational leadership in STEM
